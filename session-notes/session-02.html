Session 2

16-03-2022

Location

Warehouse, Industry Ward, The Great Black

Contact

Tani

Job

Find missing crown pieces and have them re-forged

Lead

Black & blue warehouse, back of the Industry Ward

Key Events

  • Kindle 🔥 joins the party
  • Engaged the Earth Genasi and Mephits before they surrender, handing the ledger over to Kindle 🔥 to take a proper look. 
  • Sable Guards arrive and Fletcher & Kindle 🔥 move into the hidden office, while Tabitha 💀 & Miraren 🧝‍♂️ stay behind to make sure the Earth Genasi don’t betray us to the Guards. 
  • A trap door had been revealed throughout the fight, and when asked if we could enter, the Earth Genasi refused. Miraren 🧝‍♂️ killed one of them and the others ran. 
  • 😪 Short rest 😪
  • Party heads down the trap door, avoiding traps along the way. 
  • Fake gems from back in the warehouse are required to move through the first door, so Miraren 🧝‍♂️ runs back to collect them. Finds a real sapphire while looking for the fakes, it is lost in the process of opening the door
  • In the first room, there’s a riddle
Quote

Two friends stand & travel together, one nearly useless without the other

  • The answer is legs or feet, and Fletcher puts a pair of boots on the shelf beneath the door, and the door opens. 
  • The next room houses three alcoves with paintings of various deaths, some weapons, and more riddles
  • An arrow with the riddle:
Quote

A curved stick and a straight twig mean red sap and a snapped trunk

  • An axe with the riddle:
Quote

No warning of timber could have stopped the falling petals

  • And a noose with the riddle:
Quote

A fitting cravat for a poorly chosen suit

  • We had to re-enact the deaths in the paintings to get through the door. 
  • The second room housed a chest, a door, and 2 suits of armour which began moving towards us. We tried to engage but found that we didn’t appear to be doing any damage. 
  • Miraren 🧝‍♂️ opened the chest while the others kept the suits busy, and found a pair of clockwork keys. Tabitha 💀 and Miraren 🧝‍♂️ use them to wind the soldiers back into their places next to the door, which opens once they slot into place. 
  • The third room is a study and Miraren 🧝‍♂️ spots a book that looks out of place on the bookshelf. Following a hunch, Miraren 🧝‍♂️ tilts the book out of place and the bookshelf moves to reveal another room where the crown pieces are laid out on a table with a jeweller's kit. 
  • We grab the pieces and Miraren 🧝‍♂️ also gets into a locked drawer in the desk in the main room, discovering Garnet’s diary, a strange stick, and some gold and jewellery. 
  • We discover that Sapphire has been ill, and Garnet was hoping to use the crown to pay clerics to treat her.
  • We head back to the warehouse through the tunnel and stay hidden while Sable Guards toss the room above us, before heading out to find a Blacksmith. Head to the Blacksmith “By Hammer & Hand 🔨” to get the crown fixed. 
  • We notice a lot of “Missing” posters around, and Tabitha 💀 pockets one to look at later
  • Fletcher leaves the party
  • Spend the night at The Hot Candle 🍺 Inn. 
  • 😴Long rest😴

Outcome

Crown pieces found, given to blacksmith to be repaired.

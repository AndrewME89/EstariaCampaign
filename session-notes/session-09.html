  • Used Scroll of Sending to contact Iravek to determine where to drop the trade items

  • Zaarea suggests we see Alora at the bookshop Fact & Fiction 📚 for information on demon specialists. Promises that our payment for the trade route job will be delivered later
  • Party heads to The Colossal Lily Inn 🍺 to regroup and figure out what to do.
  • Miraren 🧝‍♂️ disappears for a while to search for information on where to take Evangeline, as well as a few other bits and pieces. Can arrange to have someone perform Sending, but it will cost 70gp
  • Ridley 🦁 tries to meet with the Captain of the guard, Kelrock 🐲, and arranges an appointment for the next day

  • Tabitha 💀 returns to the Temple of Olo ☠, where the High Priest is attending a sick child

  • Miraren 🧝‍♂️ has told Zoran 🗡 where the party is, and has provided proof of Evangeline’s life with hopes of figuring out exactly what the situation is

Key Events 

  • Head to Goodie's Convenience 🛒 to purchase items for the trip to The Pale Valley. Purchase bedrolls, rations etc
  • Goodie 💖 tells us of Displacer Beasts, Dragons and other beasts outside of town
  • Meet with Iravek. Trade stock is going missing - including the people tasked with transporting the goods. We’re tasked with finding out what’s going on.
  • We are given a 1-month time limit, and the reward is 200gp and a carriage with horses, which we receive immediately to aid us in the journey.
  • Pass the time until morning by visiting Becky’s mother in the Ornate Ward to find out where Becky’s father may be.
  • Gregory Smith was a message-runner for the nobility. He had no real friends and was last meant to be heading towards The Hot Candle 🍺 for a post-work drink.
  • A message from Amal 🐍 to Alaad - leader of the Sable Guard - was the last known message he was meant to deliver.
  • When returning to The Hot Candle 🍺, Dancing Fire says she last saw him a week ago.
  • When retrieving Fleur Krisk for the escort job, Loravor 😡 uses the phrase “you know why” when telling Fleur that she’s heading to The Pale Valley.

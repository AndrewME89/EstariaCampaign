Location

Key Events 

  • The party recovers from their time at The Festival of the Willow
  • Plan to collect the cart, and the Map of Alabi while hopefully finding Kindle 🔥
  • Suda is currently devoid of anyone sober
  • Kindle 🔥 is found hanging off the edge of a bridge, where Ridley 🦁 & Miraren 🧝‍♂️ push her into the water
  • The party heads to Metes & Bounds 🌍 to collect the Map of Alabi, upon looking at the map, Ridley 🦁 determines that Stella is around a week-and-a-half away
  • Marralee 😈 spends 7 gp to purchase a Map of the Giant Star and a Map of the Major Constellations
  • Pick up the cart from Stormy Windmill 🪓
  • Head to Fresh Grand Groceries general store to collect some bits & pieces for the journey
    • 1 lb. mystery meat | 5 gp
    • Cooking Utensils | 1 gp
    • Apron | 2 sp
    • Rations x 10 } 5 gp (Marralee 😈)
  • Head to the local seamstress
    • Apron embroidered with "Cook it yourself, cunt"
    • Jacket for Bonnie 🐀
    • Bonnie 🐀's cage is turned into a backpack for Marralee 😈
    • Tarp, blankets etc for the cart
    • Throw pillows x 10
    • Loose filigree
    • Vest for Poggin 🐉
    • Lingerie (Kindle 🔥)
    • Mankini (Miraren 🧝‍♂️)
  • Kindle knows Kiana 😍 from her backstory!
  • The party (except Kindle 🔥) gains 1 level in Undercommon
  • Kindle 🔥 tries to contact Zoran 🗡 but is met with no answer
  • Ridley 🦁 attempts to make contact through sending, he only receives heavy breathing in return
  • There is immediate concern regarding Zoran 🗡's safety. Ridley 🦁 asks Goodie 💖 to look in on Zoran 🗡
  • Kindle thinks long and hard about how she knows Kiana 😍, remembers that they met in Akai and seemed to be friendly with one another and were with someone else
  • Miraren 🧝‍♂️ stays up a little to ask Kiana 😍 about how to get to The Garnet Hand, and she offers to take him there once everyone has gone to sleep. She mentions a library that Miraren 🧝‍♂️ can investigate while passing the time. Miraren 🧝‍♂️ wakes Ridley 🦁 & Marralee 😈 to investigate the library,
  • After a brief chat with Kiana 😍 about history, Ridley 🦁 reveals the Recreated map, circa 2156, Constellation Dynasty 🌍, while Miraren 🧝‍♂️ presents the Ancient Coin, circa 2156, Constellation Dynasty 💰.
  • Kiana 😍 says she's been searching for information like this for decades
  • Overall Ridley 🦁 & Marralee 😈 find nothing of interest and head back to bed
  • Miraren 🧝‍♂️ looks through the library for information on Archfey, hoping to Find information on Twiggy 🌳, but is unsuccessful. Kiana 😍 mentions that she knows of an Archfey that abandoned the Night Court to mingle with mortals and then disappeared, but doesn't know the Archfey's name
  • Kiana 😍 & Miraren 🧝‍♂️ head to The Garnet Hand. A stocky duergar named Percy guards the door.
  • Miraren 🧝‍♂️ asks around for information on the mysterious prisms, discovering that a nobleman in The Dominion of Kegal has one, though disappears without a trace whenever someone gets near him. The Garnet Hand has made no attempt to retrieve the prism in 50 years. The job is offered to Miraren 🧝‍♂️, though he never asks about a reward or confirms any interest in the job
  • Sedvioth Crofkeve 🧥 is not an approachable man
  • In the morning, Goodie 💖 just shows up in Kiana 😍's house with news about Zoran 🗡. The Aasimar has been conscripted to Akai to fight in the war, though he is currently MIA. Alaad is preparing to head off to the war, leaving Carnis and Malen in The Great Black to train new soldiers
  • Ridley 🦁 contacts Mesta 🏹 in the hopes that the God can find Zoran 🗡, but the Gods cannot see into Akai
  • The party heads through the mines and out onto the roads of Alabi, where Miraren 🧝‍♂️ plays the Wooden Pan Flute 🎺 as they travel. An odd growl and some shuffling are heard...
